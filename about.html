ABOUT SCHOOL

DR.B.R AMBADKAR GURUKULAM, also known as residential schools, are educational institutions that provide accommodation and education to students from marginalized communities.

These schools are typically run by the government , including academic instruction, vocational training, and extracurricular activities.

Social welfare schools play an important role in promoting social mobility and providing opportunities for students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

They help to break down barriers to education and provide students with the support they need to succeed in their studies and in life.
